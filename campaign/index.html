During pre-launch, no payment is collected on this page. If you submit interest or sign up for notifications, you may unsubscribe at any time.

2. During the live campaign

If the campaign is hosted on a crowdfunding platform, pledge modifications or cancellations may be available through the platform’s own settings while the campaign is live. Any platform fees or rules related to cancellations are handled according to that platform’s terms.

3. After the campaign ends

After campaign completion, funds are typically allocated to production and fulfillment preparation. Refund requests after the campaign may be reviewed case-by-case depending on production status, fulfillment stage, and platform policy.

Once shipping or fulfillment has started, refunds may no longer be possible, or may require return handling depending on the situation. If delivery fails due to an incorrect address provided by the supporter, additional shipping costs may apply for re-delivery.

For refund or support requests, contact us via the official support/contact method listed on our website. We will respond as soon as practical; response times may vary during high-volume periods.
