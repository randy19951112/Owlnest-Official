Owlnest Customer Policy & Product Use Terms

Owlnest lamps and related products are lifestyle lighting products designed to support a calm nighttime environment and bedtime routine. They are intended for ambient lighting and comfort use only, and individual experiences may vary.

1) Scope and Official Support

This Policy applies to customers who purchase Owlnest products through Owlnest’s official sales channels, including (but not limited to) our official website and other channels designated by Owlnest from time to time.

For official support, warranty, returns, refunds, and order-related matters, please contact: owlnestpq2025@gmail.com.

Please read and follow all product instructions, safety guidance, and the user manual. Use only compatible accessories and power sources as specified. Do not misuse the product, expose it to unsafe conditions, or attempt unauthorized modification, disassembly, or repair.

Owlnest does not provide medical advice and does not make medical guarantees, treatment claims, or promises regarding insomnia, sleep disorders, or any health condition. Our products are not medical devices. If you have medical concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional before use.

Delivery date is determined by the carrier’s delivery confirmation records.

  • For non-defective returns, return shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility.
  • Returned items must be complete (product, packaging, accessories, inserts, and gifts). Missing items or significant damage may affect eligibility or refund amount, subject to assessment and applicable law.

6) Defects and Replacement (Hybrid Process)

If a clear functional defect occurs within 7 days of delivery confirmation, Owlnest may, after basic verification, ship a replacement unit first and then coordinate return/recovery handling as needed. After 7 days, the item may require inspection prior to repair or replacement determination, subject to applicable law.

  • Warranty generally excludes damage or failure resulting from misuse, accidents, drops, water exposure, unauthorized modification or disassembly, improper operation, or use of incompatible accessories/power sources, to the extent permitted by law.
