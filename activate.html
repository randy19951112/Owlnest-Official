What is an Activation Token?

It’s a one-time code that confirms your purchase and securely binds this product to your account. After activation, we may only show a shortened version for privacy.

Token format example

Your token may include letters, numbers, and dashes. Example:

OWL-7F3A-9K2M-4D8Q-1X6P

Tip: Copy and paste to avoid mistakes. Spaces at the beginning/end can cause errors.

Privacy & visibility

Your Activation Token is used only to complete activation. For security, the full token is not displayed publicly and won’t appear in your product list. Keep the token in a safe place until activation is complete.
